Avengers

Avengers: Infinity War is a 2018 American superhero movie based on Marvel Comics' superhero team Avengers. The film is produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures, is a sequel to the Superhero Squad (2012) and Avengers: Ultron Empire (2015), and is also a movie. 19 in the movie series of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). The work is directed by brothers Anthony and Joe Russo, with the script written by Christopher Markus and Stephen McFeely. The film stars Robert Downey Jr., Chris Hemsworth, Mark Ruffalo, Chris Evans, Scarlett Johansson, Benedict Cumberbatch, Don Cheadle, Tom Holland, Chadwick Boseman, Paul Bettany, Elizabeth Olsen, Anthony Mackie, Sebastian Stan , Danai Gurira, Letitia Wright, Dave Bautista, Zoe Saldana, Josh Brolin and Chris Pratt. In Avengers: Infinity War, the Avengers teamed up with the Guardians of the Galaxy to prevent Thanos from collecting the full 6 Infinity Stones and destroying half of the creatures in the universe.

Malvel

Marvel Entertainment, LLC is an American entertainment company founded in June 1998 and headquartered in New York City, founded by the merger of Marvel Entertainment Group, Inc. and ToyBiz. The company is a wholly owned subsidiary of The Walt Disney Company, and is primarily known by its smaller units Marvel Comics, Marvel Animation and Marvel Television. Marvel Studios, formerly under the Marvel umbrella, has become a subsidiary of The Walt Disney Studios, where the company develops and produces a shared universe of movies that shares continuity with several the show is produced by Marvel Television.

EndGame

EndGame is an American superhero movie released in 2019, produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures. The film is the fourth installment of the Avengers franchise, after the Superhero Squad (2012), Avengers: Ultron Empire (2015) and Avengers: Infinity War (2018). This is the 22nd film of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), and is the penultimate film of the third phase in this movie universe. Director is the Russo Brothers, screenwriters are Christopher Markus and Stephen McFeely. The characters in the movie are based on the Avengers Squad of the Marvel comics. The title Avengers: Endgame is officially announced and the first trailer for the movie is officially posted online on December 7, 2018. In the film, the surviving members of the Avengers team and their allies match up. work together to reverse the damage done by Thanos in Avengers: Infinity War.
